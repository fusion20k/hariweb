Hari is Powered by YOU

Hari's translation system gets smarter with every person who uses it. When you browse with Hari, you're not just learning - you're contributing to a community-driven translation engine that improves for everyone.

The more people use Hari, the faster and more accurate the translations become. Every webpage visit helps train the system to understand context better, recognize idioms, and deliver more natural Tagalog. Your learning journey makes the next person's journey even better.

Learn Your Way

Control your immersion level with a simple slider from 0-100%. Just starting out? Set it lower to see more English mixed in with Tagalog, making it easier to understand while you're building your vocabulary. You'll never feel completely lost.

As you get better, slide it higher for more complete immersion. At 100%, every word is Tagalog. You can adjust your level anytime, moving at your own pace as your confidence grows.
