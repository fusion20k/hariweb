Hari Gets Smarter With Every Reader

Hari learns from every page you visit, so translations get more accurate, more natural, and more idiomatic over time. As more learners use Hari, the system better understands context and expressions, and everyone benefits from cleaner Tagalog.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't Hari completely free?

Each page you translate uses paid translation APIs. The $20/month subscription covers those costs so we can keep Hari ad-free and focused on quality.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can downgrade or cancel in a couple of clicks—no long-term commitment.

Which sites does Hari work on?

Hari works on most, if not all, text-based websites, including news sites, blogs, and many learning platforms. Some platforms with heavy DRM (like certain streaming services) may be limited.
