1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Hari ("Hari", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Hari browser extension and related websites (collectively, the "Service").

By installing or using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Controller and Contact Details

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, Hari is operated by an individual developer (the "Controller").

You can contact the Controller at:

  • Email: support@haritagalog.com
  • Postal address: [POSTAL ADDRESS TO BE ADDED]

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

1. Account and Subscription Information

  • Email address
  • User ID and authentication data
  • Subscription status (e.g., free, active paid, cancelled)
  • Stripe customer ID and subscription ID (but not full payment card details)

2. Extension Settings and Usage Information

  • Immersion level and other in-extension preferences
  • Whether Hari is enabled or disabled on particular sites
  • Technical information required for the extension to function (such as feature flags or configuration values)

3. Content Processed for Translation

  • Text content from web pages you visit to the extent necessary to perform translations into Tagalog
  • Language and configuration parameters associated with the translation request

4. Technical and Log Data

  • IP address, browser type and version, and basic device information as transmitted in normal HTTP(S) requests
  • Timestamps and error logs related to use of the Service

We do not intentionally collect passwords, payment card numbers, or other sensitive information from web pages, and the extension is designed to avoid processing fields that are typically used for such data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including translating website content into Tagalog.
  • To manage user accounts, authenticate users, and determine subscription status.
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe.
  • To secure, monitor, and troubleshoot the Service, including preventing abuse and diagnosing technical issues.
  • To communicate with you about the Service, including important updates, security notices, and administrative messages.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") or similar laws apply, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: To provide and operate the Service and manage your subscription.
  • Legitimate interests: To secure and improve the Service, prevent abuse, and respond to your communications, where such interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with accounting, tax, and other legal requirements.

6. How We Process Translation Data

When you enable Hari on a webpage, the extension reads the text content of that page and sends the relevant text to our translation infrastructure, which uses the Lara API to generate Tagalog translations.

This text is used solely for the purpose of generating and returning translations and is not used for unrelated profiling or advertising.

7. Use of Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with the following categories of third parties, only to the extent necessary to operate the Service:

1. Supabase

We use Supabase to host our database and authentication. Supabase stores account and subscription-related information such as email address, user ID, and subscription status.

2. Stripe

We use Stripe as our payment processor. Stripe processes your payment information, including card details, in accordance with Stripe's own privacy policy. We receive limited information from Stripe, such as the last four digits of the card, card brand, and subscription status.

3. Lara API

We use Lara API solely to perform text translation. Page text and associated translation parameters are sent to Lara API to generate Tagalog translations, which are returned to your browser.

4. Infrastructure Providers

We may use hosting, storage, and logging providers that process technical data necessary to deliver and secure the Service.

Each third-party provider processes data on our behalf under appropriate contractual safeguards.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service to you.
  • Maintain accurate records of your subscription and payments.
  • Comply with our legal obligations and resolve disputes.

When personal data is no longer required for these purposes, we will delete or anonymize it within a reasonable time.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed on servers located outside of your state, province, or country, including in jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws.

Where required by law, we implement appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to ensure that your personal data receives an adequate level of protection.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Right to data portability (to receive certain data in a machine-readable format).
  • Right to withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@haritagalog.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

11. Rights of California Residents

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") or similar laws, including:

  • The right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • The right to request deletion of personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • The right to opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" of personal information, to the extent applicable.

Hari does not sell personal information for money. If you wish to exercise any California privacy rights, contact us at support@haritagalog.com.

12. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including encryption in transit and access controls on our systems.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can delete the information.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates when it was last revised.

If changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify you, such as by posting a notice on our website or through the extension. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
